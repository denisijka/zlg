Our company

For over 20 years, ZIM Logistics has been one of the leading logistics service providers. With an extensive network of 8 offices located in China, Canada, USA, Brazil, Hong Kong, Taiwan, Singapore, Vietnam, and our head office in Israel, ZIM Logistics specializes in handling a wide range of freight in all shapes and sizes. We have the logistics expertise to effectively integrate transportation with your supply chain and handle all of your freight forwarding needs by connecting your cargo to various destination around the world.

The array of services offered by ZIM Logistics Vietnam ensures supply chain success for our customers’ peace of mind. With us, you will receive unlimited freight services, compliance expertise and 24/7 support from our dedicated staff to manage your domestic and international transport needs.

Here are some key benefits of working with us!

  • ZIM Logistics Vietnam is located in Vietnam’s four major cities including Ho Chi Minh City, Ha Noi, Hai Phong and Da Nang
  • With over 65 employees, we generate an annual total of 10,000 TEU to various destinations around the world
  • We export and import a monthly volume of over 200 containers to/from India, the Indian Subcontinent, the Middle East, South-East Asia, and China
  • ZL Vietnam is a strategic partner of Amazon, providing tailor made solutions for eCommerce businesses
