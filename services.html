Customs Brokerage

ZIM Logistics Vietnam’s expert knowledge in customs procedures means that we can provide customs clearance services to all our customers. This includes assistance in processing declarations through customs and associated border agencies for both trading and non-trading cargo. We’ll also prepare all the required documentation and certification, calculate duties, and of course, take care of the delivery for direct, transit or shipment returns. Our customs brokerage services all also available for any of your eCommerce logistics needs.

There are multiple paths for optimizing your logistics processes, which is why, in addition to our customs brokerage services, we also offer various consulting services for setting up your import or export licenses, factory arrangements or organizing required authority inspections.
